I'm Lucas Burridge — a 2D puppet rigging artist obsessed with the mechanics of believable character performance. My rigs aren't just technical setups; they're tools built for animators to love using.

Whether it's a bouncy mascot in Moho Pro, a production-ready character or prop in ToonBoom Harmony, or a motion graphics hero in After Effects — I build rigs that are intuitive and expressive.

Every joint, every deformer, every layer order — obsessively considered.
